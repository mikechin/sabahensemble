The Sabah Ensemble will be performing at the 35th Annual Rakkasah Middle Eastern Dance Festival on Sunday, March 15. The Rakkasah is a two-and-a-half day festival featuring two stages of dancing, classes, and vendors selling music, clothing, and jewelry.
