Belly Dance From the Inside Out

A contemporary approach to the ancient dance form of belly dancing

"When I belly dance, I feel the deepest sources of energy within myself. The movements allow me to celebrate the integration of my body, mind, and spirit. From the movements I receive a sense of wholeness, health, well-being. When I belly dance, my body feels beautiful."
"A good belly dancer must always have dignity and an ability to express the many aspects of life: sorrow and pain as well as happiness and joy."
Jamie Miller "Sabah" (1942-1999)

The Sabah Ensemble, founded by Jamie Miller ("Sabah"), performs and teaches belly dancing as a contemporary art. Using the classic form of belly dancing as a foundation, we incorporate the use of modern techniques – creative body alignment/ideokinesis, improvisation, visualization, energy awareness, and acting exercises – to create a unique style of movement and connection.

We offer a weekly dance class [LINK TO CLASS PAGE]. You can find announcements about class as well as information on our performances here [LINK TO NEWS PAGE].

We hope to share the beauty of this timeless dance with you and that it will enrich your life as it has ours.
